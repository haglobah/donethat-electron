Active applications
This allows us to know which apps are active. This helps to understand what happens in between screenshots. On macOS, you may see a permission request to control your browser to capture the active URL.

Window Tracking Permission Required

DoneThat needs permission to track active windows.

On Linux:

  • Grant accessibility permissions in your system settings
  • For Wayland sessions, some window tracking features may be limited
  • Ensure DoneThat has permission to access window information

Note: You are on Wayland. Application detection doesn't work for most apps due to its security architecture. Your data will be less accurate.

Audio permissions

These inputs help us understand your work better but are not required. You can enable or disable them at any time.

Microphone
We check if your microphone is active to see if you are in a meeting. During meetings, we process your voice to understand what you are working on.
Meeting audio
Also record computer sound (meetings, videos) when the microphone is active.
On some Wayland systems, enabling this can trigger a screen-sharing dialog at the start of each meeting, and system audio capture can also be unreliable.

Audio Session Detection Tool Required

DoneThat needs pactl to detect when your microphone is active.

This tool is required for audio session detection on Linux. After installation, restart DoneThat.

App masking

Mask apps with gray fill to protect sensitive data. Use keywords to ignore specific windows or fully exclude an app's activity. Use this for meeting apps (Zoom, Teams, etc.) to keep them private.

Bring your own LLM (optional)

Process screenshots and other raw data with your own LLM. Keys are encrypted and stored on your device. This only affects raw data. The processed data will still be synced to our servers and further analzed there to eg detect tasks.

You can further increase privacy by deleting intermediary data at the end of every day automatically in your settings in the dashboard.

Note that raw data shared with the chat will also be processed on our servers (and deleted after).

Gemini API Key

Add your Google Gemini API key to process raw data directly with Gemini.

OpenAI-compatible API

Configure an OpenAI-compatible API endpoint. Local or cloud. Used when Gemini key is not available.

Note: Use a multimodal model that supports image and audio inputs (e.g. GPT-4o, Gemini). Need help? Visit our support page.
